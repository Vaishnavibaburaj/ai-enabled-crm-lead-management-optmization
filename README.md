AI-Enabled CRM Lead Management – BA Case Study

This project analyzes CRM lead management inefficiencies using SQL and evaluates the impact of rule-based AI lead prioritization.

AS-IS Analysis

Using PostgreSQL, baseline performance was measured:

Overall Conversion Rate: 33%

Close Rate: 50%

Early-stage inefficiencies identified in lead qualification and prioritization.

AI Enhancement

A rule-based scoring model (based on Lead Source and Budget) classified leads into High, Medium, and Low priority.

Prioritized strategy improved conversion from 33% to 50% — a 17 percentage-point increase.

Visualization

Key performance metrics and AI impact were presented through Power BI dashboards.

Tools

PostgreSQL | Power BI | BRD/FRD | UAT | Process Modeling
